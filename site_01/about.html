Meet Sean Park

We give each client and case the attention they deserve.”

Sean Park

Founder & Managing Partner

In his 15 years of practicing law, Sean has handled thousands of DUI cases and has a proven track record of success in the courtroom on behalf of his clients in DUI cases, other criminal defense matters as well as plaintiff’s personal injury cases.

As a former prosecutor, Sean would frequently instruct prosecutors as well as police officers on the topic of presenting effective and compelling testimony in the courtroom on DUI and other types of cases. During his time in private practice, Sean frequently gives lectures to other lawyers on the topics of DUI, trial presentation and effective jury selection. Sean also regularly appears as a legal analyst on CNN networks, CBS Atlanta 46 and has been featured on nationally syndicated news and entertainment programs discussing legal matters in current events.

Over the course of his career, Sean has acquired extensive experience at all levels of court in the states of Tennessee and Georgia. Additionally, Sean strives to develop and maintain good working relationships with judges, prosecutors and their office staff. In some cases, these working relationships date back to the beginning of Sean’s practice of law in 1999.

Sean is licensed and admitted to practice law before all state courts in the states of Tennessee and Georgia, including the Supreme Court and Court of Appeals. He is also licensed to practice in the State of Florida.
